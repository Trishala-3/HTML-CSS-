About Us


Studio ghibili is a japanese animation studio in Tokyo.It has strong portfolio and it include various various media format such as television commercials and television films Studio ghibili was founded on june 15 1985 by Hayao miyazaki,Isao Takahata and producer Toshio Suzuki.The studio has collaborated with video game studio on the visual development of several games.The name "Ghibili"was chosen by miyazaki from the italian noun ghibili based on Libyan Arabic name for hot desert wind .The studio was founded after the success of the 1984 film Nausicaa of the valley of the wind .suzuki was part of the film production team,and founded studio ghibili with miyazaki,who also invited takahata to join them. The studio has mainly produced films by miyazaki,with the second most prolific director being takahata. In August 1996 ,The walt Disney Company and Tokuma shoten formed a partnership wherein Walt disney studios would be the sole international distributor for Tokuma shoten's studio ghibili animated films. On June 1 1997 Tokuma Shoten publishing consolidated its media operations by merging studio ghibili ,Tokuma shoten intermediate software and Tokuma international under one location. In october 2001 the ghibili mueseum opened in tokyo.It contains exhibits based on studio ghibili films and shows animations,including a number of short studio ghibili not available.
